Enbio

Unleashing a green revolution where eco-enthusiasm meets digital brilliance, transforming universities into sustainable splendor, one app at a time.

enbio.com

Background

Enbio is an innovative sustainability application designed specifically for universities, aimed at fostering a greener environment. It focuses on five key areas: Recycling, E-waste management, Carpooling, Event Organizing, and Veggie Patch.

Students and staff actively participate by separating recyclables and earning rewards, connecting and sharing rides to reduce carbon emissions and traffic congestion, exchanging or donating used tech, and utilizing food waste for organic farming. The platform also highlights achievements and keeps users informed about upcoming sustainability-related events.

Design Outcomes

Embrace Enbio, which intertwines sustainability with student life, offering an enjoyable university experience. Its captivating user interface allures with interactivity and vibrant color themes, each representing a distinct system aspect.

The abstract design techniques deviate from the conventional, aiming to engage users and attract new enthusiasts. Let's advocate for universities to embrace these practices, nurturing Enbio into a fully functional application.

Wow, Enbio's innovative approach to tackling challenges like parking congestion and air contamination at SLIIT's campus in Malabe is truly commendable. Their solution is nothing short of remarkable for our university community. The inclusion of user rewards adds an exciting element, elevating the overall experience and attracting new users. Enbio has proven to be an incredible solution to one of our university's most pressing problems. Kudos to the Enbio team for their outstanding work!
